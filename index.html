About Us

Matlok is a full service digital marketing agency located in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada. Matlok Marketing specializes in digital advertising, website design and development, brand development, social media, email management, marketing strategy, brand photography, creative campaign direction and more. Matlok delivers through our lead generation, increased brand presence, online engagement, and a renewed inspiration in your brand. Our team of experts are eager to work with you.
